Overview

Implemented a complete IEEE 802.11 WiFi receiver at the physical layer from scratch in MATLAB, processing raw complex IQ samples through to decoded data bits without using any WiFi toolboxes.

The pipeline covers every step of the OFDM reception process: from initial packet detection in raw samples through timing and frequency correction, channel estimation using known training symbols, subcarrier equalization, and final bit recovery.

Receiver Chain
01Packet detection — energy detection and autocorrelation on raw IQ samples
02Coarse timing synchronization using the short training sequence
03Fine timing synchronization and fractional sample offset correction
04Carrier frequency offset (CFO) estimation and correction
05Channel estimation from long training symbols via LS estimator
06OFDM demodulation — FFT, pilot extraction, and per-subcarrier equalization
07Symbol demapping and bitstream output
